Biriyani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef, prawn, or fish) and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are also added

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • Meat
  • Basmati Rice
  • Bay leaf
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • Onions
  • Turmeric
  • Chilli Powder

Steps

  1. Put the boiling water in a saucepan,
  2. add the washed rice
  3. boil for 5 minutes and drain
  4. Layer the chicken mixture with the rice starting with the rice then the chicken mixture and repeat this twice
  5. Garnish with cumin seeds, and ginger
